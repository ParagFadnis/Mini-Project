📘 Animal Safety Guidelines

Learn how to safely interact with animals

✅ Do's

  • Feed animals safely with appropriate food
  • Maintain a safe distance from wild animals
  • Call professionals for injured animals
  • Provide water in hot weather
  • Report animal abuse immediately

❌ Don'ts

  • Never harm or abuse animals
  • Don't panic near injured animals
  • Avoid sudden movements around strays
  • Don't feed chocolate or toxic foods
  • Never separate babies from mothers
